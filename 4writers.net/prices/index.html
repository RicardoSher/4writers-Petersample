Our Rates

4writers.net goes to great lengths to employ the most qualified writers specializing in different subjects. By endeavoring to teach our writers, we remunerate our personnel with a well-deserved pay, additional bonuses, and tips. High rates per page and timely salary transfers are what we promise to our dedicated writers!

The order’s salary is defined by several criteria:

Delivery Time

How much time is given to complete the order

Order’s Level

How difficult was the order - High school, College, University, etc.

Pages

Number of pages to be written

Your writing level will be determined by the results of the test that you are asked to complete when registering at our website. Test results will also influence limit for number of orders you will be able to take at a time.

Writer's level can only be upgraded once the writer's performance during the set period of time has been improved. The upgrade of writer's level depends on: the amount of completed orders, customers' feedback, Quality Assurance Department's

reports and general writer`s performance records (absence of excessive revisions, late deliveries, reassignments).

Once you have taken the order, you automatically agree to all the requirements of that order (i.e. number of pages, deadline, instructions, etc.). Your salary will be sent to you once per month. The salary will be released within 7 days after it is calculated (approximately after 15-18th) for all orders completed between the 1st and the 30th (31st) of the previous month.
