1. How do I know whether I am eligible to apply for a job at 4Writers.net?

You are welcome to sign up and work for 4Writers.net if you:

  • Posses excellent English writing skills
  • Hold Bachelor’s, Master’s, or a PhD degree
  • Have access to the Internet
  • Are a responsible employee

2. What should I do to sign up?

Please, fill in the sign up form on our website (you may also provide a sample paper written by) and pass our online test. Please be ready to verify your identity (to avoid possible issues with online fraud, your HR manager may ask you to send a copy of ID. Once our specialists process the information submitted by you and check how successfully you coped with the test, you will be contacted by our HR and guided as to the further actions.

3. If I have a full-time job, am I still eligible to apply?

People working for our company are basically freelance writers. It allows you to take as many orders as you can complete successfully and you decide when you can do that. Most of our writers do have other jobs and still keep working for our company. Therefore, you can have both. We do care about one thing though – the quality of your writing should remain high. If the quality of writing is not compromised and you submit plagiarism-free papers on time, you can simultaneously have as many jobs as you want.

5. Once I am approved to work for your company, will it be reflected on my official job status?

No, it won’t. We are offering a freelance writing opportunities and well-organized, joint partnership in which both parties have both rights and responsibilities. 8. Is there any limit to the number of orders I can take?

There is no limit for taking orders per month. However, there is a limit orders that you can take and work on simultaneously. This limit can be later increased or decreased depending on your writer’s performance. Thus, it will be totally up to you to decide how many orders you want to work on. Yet, please, keep in mind: the more orders you successfully complete, the more money you get. Many of our writers work hard for one month and then they take a break for few days/weeks to relax. So, there is no pressure from us to take X number of orders. You have a full control over your working schedule by choosing the number of orders you want to work on.

9. What kinds of orders are available?

There are hundreds of subjects and topics available every day in the list of orders. It's only up to you to choose the orders that interest you. You can make your choice based on your field of expertise, deadline, number of pages, etc. So, it is you who really make all those choices. Our available orders usually include essays, research papers, custom writings, dissertations, proofreading and even editing. If you perform well and receive positive feedback from our clients, you can be sure to receive extra bonuses.

10. What about the privacy policy regarding writers’ personal information?

All personal information that you provide is stored at our secure internal database. Our clients or any other third party will never come to know anything about your personal details. We do understand the importance of the privacy policy within the industry and, therefore, we do protect your personal information.
